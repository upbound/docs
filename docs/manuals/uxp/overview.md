Upbound Crossplane (UXP) is the AI-native distribution of [Crossplane][crossplane] by Upbound. Crossplane is a framework for building your own [control plane][control-plane].

UXP is what Upbound uses in its control planes-as-a-service Spaces hosting environment. Users can also [deploy][self-managed-uxp] a self-managed installation of UXP directly on a Kubernetes cluster.

UXP is available to any users under Upbound's Community plan. Commercial licenses are available. See [license management][licensing] for more details.

:::tip

Read the [Get Started][get-started] guide to learn how to use UXP to build your own control plane.

:::
